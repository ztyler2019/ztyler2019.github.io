About Me

I graduated from UC Davis with a Physics B.S. in 2019 and aquired a job as a Data Engineer at HQ Sofware Consulting one year later in 2020. I began tapping into my creative side in 2018, starting with drawing and ending with my discovery of Blender in 2019. I am lucky enough to have a talented group of friends, and we assembled the Youtube group Ban Letter, a channel dedicated to surreal scenarios unable to be recreated in a real-world setting. Together, we are steadily gaining subscribers and are continuing to create new content. Each one more challenging than the next.
