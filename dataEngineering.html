Projects

Below is a list of projects I have completed:

Technology Recovery Protocol (TRP)

Our State government client requires a full list of procedures to be updated annually in order to ensure minimal down-time during emergency situations. I was tasked with coordinating between multiple teams to assemble a master document, reporting backup, recovery and testing procedures. With the assistance of 20 team members, I uploaded a 150 page document detailing essential technology needed to maintain function. I submitted this document onto the State's internal Drupal site that my company developed for the State to create living documents that can be modified on the fly, as well as give specific users the ability to edit sections that they were involved in creating. This implementation has sped up the annual report timeline by sectioning the documentation responsibility by employee role.
